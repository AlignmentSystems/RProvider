RProvider requires a reference to RDotNet - http://rdotnet.codeplex.com/.  This is not included in the repo because it is covered by LGPL.

Please download the tip version of RDotNet from here: http://rdotnet.codeplex.com/SourceControl/list/changesets.  
Place binaries in the References subdirectory of this project, and then build.

We hope to make this simpler in the future!
